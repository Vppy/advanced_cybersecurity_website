Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to a security incident?

Our incident response team is available 24/7/365. We typically begin remote response within 1 hour of notification and can deploy on-site teams within 24 hours for critical incidents.

Do you offer customized security solutions for specific industries?

Yes, we specialize in developing industry-specific security solutions for sectors including finance, healthcare, government, manufacturing, and retail, each with unique compliance requirements and threat profiles.

How do you ensure the security of cloud environments?

Our cloud security approach includes cloud-native security controls, configuration management, identity and access management, data protection, and continuous monitoring tailored to all major cloud providers.

What certifications do your security professionals hold?

Our team includes professionals certified in CISSP, CISM, CEH, OSCP, AWS/Azure/GCP security specializations, and many other relevant industry certifications to ensure the highest level of expertise.
